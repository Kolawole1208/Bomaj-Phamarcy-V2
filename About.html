Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Benjamin Omaj, Bomaj Pharmacist International began as a single pharmacy in Lagos, Nigeria. Today, we operate across 24 countries with over 300 facilities worldwide.

Our journey from a local pharmacy to a global healthcare solutions provider reflects our commitment to innovation, quality, and accessibility in pharmaceutical care.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, innovative pharmaceutical solutions that improve health outcomes worldwide through cutting-edge research and compassionate care.

Our Vision

To be the leading global pharmaceutical partner, transforming healthcare delivery through technology, research, and patient-centric solutions by 2030.
